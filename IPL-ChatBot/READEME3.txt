# IPL Chatbot UI

This repository contains a simple webpage UI to interact with the IPL ChatBot hosted on Amazon Lex.

## Files
- `index3.html` : The UI to interact with the chatbot.
- `ipl_2019_dataset` : XLS Worksheet contains the actual data of IPL matches
- `README3.md` : This setup guide.

### Steps to Run Locally:
1. Open `index3.html` in your browser.
2. Interact with the chatbot by typing your questions related to IPL matches, toss, venue, player of the match, etc., and the bot will fetch the relevant data.
3. You can ask questions like Who won the match, Who won the toss, In which venue the match happened, Who is the player of the match.
4. You have to clearly give input of which 2 teams played the match and on which date the match happened to get the correct output.
	
### Sample Questions:
1. Who won the match between Royal Challengers Bangalore and Chennai Super Kings on 2019-03-23?
2. Who won the match on march 24 2019 ? -- (Sunrisers Hyderabad, Kolkata Knight Riders)
3. In which venue the match between Delhi Capitals and Mumbai Indians happened? -- (2019-03-24)
4. Who won the toss between Kings XI Punjab and Rajasthan Royals ? -- (March 25, 2019)
5. Who is the player of the match in the game between Chennai Super Kings and Rajasthan Royals ? -- (31/03/19)
